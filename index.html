Who We Are

CONTRACT.
EXECUTE.
DELIVER.

Orinoco Energy Group Inc is a U.S.-incorporated energy logistics company formed in March 2026 — at the exact moment the OFAC reactivation window opened for Venezuela. We were built for this moment.

We structure and win contracts for project cargo and energy infrastructure logistics across the Americas, then execute through a fully integrated group that controls every node of the supply chain: origin, port, customs, and final delivery.

Our operational backbone has been at Puerto de Guanta since 2018 — the only port in eastern Venezuela capable of receiving Orinoco Belt project cargo, including equipment exceeding 300 tons.

Work With Us
The Problem We Solve

Every oil major and EPC firm moving equipment to Venezuela faces the same challenge: no single, OFAC-compliant U.S. entity that can win the logistics contract AND guarantee ground-level execution. Orinoco is that entity.

Why Now

OFAC General Licenses 46B, 47, 48A, 30B, and 52 — issued January–March 2026 with no expiration — have opened the legal framework for U.S. entities to operate fully in Venezuela's energy sector. We are positioned, compliant, and operational.

Our Structure

Orinoco wins the contract. OEG Internacional moves the cargo. OCK Servicios Logísticos works the port. ADUAVEN clears customs. One group. One accountability. Zero gaps.

What We Do

END-TO-END
ENERGY LOGISTICS

From U.S. origin to Venezuelan project site — we coordinate every link in the chain.

01 — CONTRACT STRUCTURING
Contract Origination & Negotiation

We engage directly with oil majors, EPC companies, and EXIM Bank-financed exporters to structure project cargo and logistics contracts for Venezuela operations. We are the U.S. counterparty.

02 — INTERNATIONAL FREIGHT
Vessel Chartering & Ocean Freight

Full and partial vessel charters for project and breakbulk cargo. FCL/LCL from all U.S. and Venezuelan ports. NVOCC & IAC licensed. Air freight coordination. Direct shipping line connections — no intermediaries.

03 — PORT OPERATIONS
Puerto de Guanta — Heavy Cargo

Licensed port operations via OCK Servicios Logísticos. Stevedoring, heavy-lift up to 300+ tons, RoRo, container handling, vessel agency. The only operator in eastern Venezuela executing at this scale.

04 — CUSTOMS CLEARANCE
SENIAT-Registered Customs

Full customs clearance via ADUAVEN, our affiliated SENIAT-registered broker. DAI/SDR filings, tariff classification, pre-valuation, special regimes. Group-owned — no third-party exposure.

05 — INLAND DELIVERY
Land Transport to the Faja

Overland transport coordination from Puerto de Guanta to Faja del Orinoco project sites, eastern Venezuelan refineries, and cryogenic facilities. Route-planned for oversized loads.

06 — WAREHOUSING
Storage & Inventory Management

Cargo storage, consolidation, and inventory management at facilities in Miami (FL), Caracas, and Barcelona (Venezuela). Picking, packing, cross-docking, refrigerated storage, and vehicle storage available.
